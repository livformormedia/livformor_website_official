Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Is ketamine therapy safe?

Yes. Ketamine has been FDA-approved as an anesthetic since 1970 and has an excellent safety profile. At sub-anesthetic doses used for depression, side effects are minimal and temporary. You're monitored by our medical team throughout every session.

How quickly will I feel results?

Many patients report noticeable improvement within 24-48 hours of their first infusion. This is dramatically faster than traditional antidepressants which can take 4-6 weeks. A full treatment series (typically 6 infusions) provides the most lasting results.

What does the treatment feel like?

Most patients describe it as a deeply relaxing experience. You may feel a sense of floating or mild dissociation, which fades within 15-20 minutes after the infusion ends. Many patients say it feels like a "pause button" for their anxious thoughts.

How much does ketamine therapy cost?

We offer competitive pricing for the Chicago area. During your free consultation, we'll provide a complete cost breakdown and discuss financing options. We believe cost should never be a barrier to getting the help you need.

Can I drive home after treatment?

No ‚Äî you'll need someone to drive you home after each session. The effects of the infusion wear off within about an hour, but for your safety, we require a designated driver or rideshare arrangement.
